About Our Charity


DR TANJILUR & MEHRUN NESA FOUNDATION is a nonprofit development organization charity to improving lives across a dynamic and developing BANGLADESH. We've been organizing Some social events and seminars for rural awareness, heath care, seminars for seniors and youth for last 20 years in our country. We are collecting funds to generate our services along with our own charitable funds throughout the people who lives under poverty line. Since 2001— we are venturing into almost every unexplored corner of our country . Informed by six decades of experience and deep local expertise, our work across the region addresses five overarching goals—strengthen governance, empower women, child care , expand economic opportunity, increase environmental awareness, and promote lifestyle to our deprived people.
Working with public and private partners, the Foundation receives funding from a diverse group of bilateral and multilateral development agencies, foundations, corporations, and individuals. In 2020, we provided $78.6 million in direct program support and distributed textbooks and other educational materials valued at $5 million.
